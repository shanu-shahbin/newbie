The NewbieOs

NewbieOs was created by software engineers Founder Shanu Shahbin and Co-Founder Akshay MV with a clear mission: to tackle the high unemployment rate among freshers in India. As of 2024, India faces an unemployment rate of around 7.5%, with freshers particularly affected. NewbieOs is here to change that by offering a platform dedicated to connecting freshers with job opportunities tailored to their skills.

Designed specifically for candidates with 0 to 2 years of experience, NewbieOs integrates over 100 job portals into one seamless experience. Our platform features handpicked jobs to ensure relevance and quality, while actively filtering out scam job listings, so you can focus on genuine opportunities. With the power of AI-driven recommendations, we help you discover jobs that match your profile, reducing the job search time from weeks to just a few hours.

NewbieOs is not just about finding jobs — it's about providing hope, streamlining the process, and helping India’s talented freshers connect with top companies and startups. Whether you’re seeking positions in IT, finance, design, or any other field, NewbieOs helps you get one step closer to your dream job.
